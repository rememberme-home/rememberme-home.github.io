Common questions

  • Are photos uploaded? No. Photos stay on your device and are not sent to us.
  • Is an account required? No account is required for the basic memorial portrait experience.
  • Can the app work offline? Yes. The core portrait experience works offline.
  • How do I remove a photo? Replace or remove it inside the app, or delete the app data from your device.
